A registered advisor with a quarter-century of conviction.

Summa is an investment advisor registered and regulated by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). Founded in 2001, the firm has accumulated more than twenty-five years of experience in advising high-net-worth families on wealth and long-term strategic counsel.

We deliver sophisticated, bespoke solutions held to the highest standards of the industry — quietly, deliberately, and with the long horizon of family capital firmly in mind.

Our work begins where brokerage relationships end — at the intersection of family, structure, and long-term wealth stewardship.
